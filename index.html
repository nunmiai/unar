Our Story

The journey of Unar (உணர்) began when a simpler, more honest path was sought. For too long, the world has relied on harsh chemicals that mask our true nature.

A deep longing was felt for the familiar scents of our daily lives—the Champa, the Frangipani, and the Vetiver that we walk past every day but rarely truly notice.

What started as a search for the "perfect mix" soon became a cherished family ritual. In the quiet blending of natural oils, a spouse and a child were brought closer together. Much like the shared joy of baking or the patience of fishing, these perfumes were crafted with love and a clear purpose.

It was discovered that nature provides everything needed to elevate the mood, without the need for synthetic spirits or artificial flavors.

Unar exists so this discovery can be shared with you. The mission is not merely to sell a product, but to inspire a more conscious choice for what touches the skin.

Feel Yourself. From our family's heart to yours—this is fragrance, realized.

What is Solid Perfume?

Solid perfume is a concentrated fragrance made from natural waxes and oils. Unlike alcohol-based perfumes, our solid perfumes are gentle on the skin, long-lasting, and perfect for on-the-go application.

How to Use:

Use the spatula to take a small amount. Glide it directly onto your pulse points (wrists, neck, behind ears) and underarms until absorbed.
